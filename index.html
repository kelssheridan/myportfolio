ABOUT ME

Welcome to my digital portfolio! My name is Kelsey Sheridan and I'm a Literature, Media, and Communications major at Georgia Tech with a passion for visual design. I’m choosing to concentrate on the threads of Interaction Design and Communications for my major.

I’ve been creating work and smearing paint and doodling over notebooks since my mom stuck a crayon in my hands when I was two. I hope to be able to translate my childhood dreams and ambitions into a real-world, successful career by transitioning the sketches of paper dolls into sketches of sophisticated designs and by transitioning snapshots of flowers growing by a sidewalk into professional shots for ad campaigns.

I hope you enjoy exploring my website and following along on the unique journey each of these projects took me!
